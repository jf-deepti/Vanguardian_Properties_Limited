Frequently Asked Question

GOT QUESTIONS?
WE'VE GOT ANSWERS

Our FAQ section is designed provide clear answer to the most common inquiries, helping you make informed decisions about your lawn care needs.

Q. What services does J&B Lawn Services offer?

A.

We offer a comprehensive range of services including mowing, trimming, weed control, fertilization, and seasonal cleanups tailored to your property's needs.

Q. Are your team members insured and experienced?

A.

Absolutely! Every member of the J&B Lawn Services team is fully trained, experienced, and insured. We take pride delivering high-quality lawn care safely and professionally.

Q. Can I customize the services for my lawn?

A.

Yes, we provide fully customizable plans. Whether you need a one-time cleanup or ongoing weekly maintenance, we adapt our services to fit your lawn's specific requirements.

Q. Do you provide landscaping design services?

A.

While our primary focus is maintenance and care, we do offer basic landscaping enhancements like mulch installation, bed defining, and seasonal flower planting.

Q. How do I maintain my lawn between services?

A.

Water deeply but infrequently (usually 1-2 times a week), avoid cutting the grass too short, and ensure your pets don't damage specific spots. Our experts will leave you with personalized tips after every visit!
