Frequently Asked Questions

HAVE QUESTIONS?
WE HAVE ANSWERS

Find answers to the most common questions about our property preservation, maintenance, handyman, and field services. We're here to help you make informed decisions with confidence.

Q. What services does Vanguardian Properties offer?

A.

We provide complete property preservation and maintenance services, including inspections, property securing, debris removal, lawn care, winterization, lock changes, repairs, cleaning, and general property maintenance.

Q. Are your team members experienced?

A.

Yes. Our experienced field team is trained to handle property preservation and maintenance projects efficiently, ensuring quality workmanship, safety, and reliable service on every job.

Q. Can I customize the services for my property?

A.

Absolutely. Every property has different needs. We can tailor our services based on your requirements, whether it's a one-time job or ongoing property maintenance.

Q. Do you provide handyman services?

A.

Yes. We offer professional handyman services, including minor repairs, fixture replacements, basic carpentry, drywall patching, painting touch-ups, and other general maintenance tasks to keep your property in excellent condition.

Q. What is your typical service timeframe?

A.

Most routine service requests are scheduled within 1–3 business days, depending on the property location and scope of work. For urgent requests, contact us directly, and we'll prioritize your service as quickly as possible.
